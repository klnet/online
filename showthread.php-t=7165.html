Greetings from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore!






We are pleased to inform you that applications for NTU undergraduate programmes for academic year 2009/2010 is now open for all Vietnam High School Graduation Certificate as well as other equivalent qualifications. Applications with outstanding academic records and extra-curricular activities are welcome to apply for ASEAN Undergraduate Scholarship, Nanyang Scholarship and other scholarships.



Our University will be holding a talk in December 2008 and we cordially invite you to attend our presentations on the opportunities available in pursuing an NTU undergraduate education



HANOI


Date: 7 Dec 2008, Sunday

Time: 9a.m.
Venue : Sofitel Plaza Hanoi

1 Thanh Nien Road

Ba Dinh District, Hanoi


HO CHI MINH


Date : 6 Dec 2008, Saturday
Time: 9a.m.

Venue: Hotel Equatorial Ho Chi Minh

242 Tran Binh Trong District 5

Ho Chi Minh City





You may wish to register to our Talk at the Hotel at the following links.

Registration is not compulsory and we warmly welcome you to join us!

· Ho Chi Minh City: www.ntu.edu.sg/oad2/HCM_admtalk.htm

· Hanoi: www.ntu.edu.sg/oad2/Hanoi_admtalk.htm

We would be grateful if you could kindly forward the e-posters to your friends who would be interested in joining NTU. Please do not hesitate to write to us should you have any further enquiries. We look forward to meeting up with you!

Quote:

The mistake here is "revolve round". We can replace it by "revolve around" (=orbit).


Quote:
**Goes & goes the time goes on we are not alone
May be... "Time goes on" is not right. It should be "Time goes by".


Quote:
Sometime we will smile sometime we will cry somehow
"Sometime" has a different meaning from "sometimes" and "some times". In this situation, I think we should use "sometimes".


Quote:
Don't forget believing yourself - Tomorrow's never die
I'm not sure, but "Don't forget to believe in yourself" may be better for this.

Have you got any PLAN for THIS SUMMER ? Join LOCAL SUPPORTING TEAM – AIESEC Hanoi to make your summer more amazing and awesome.




LOCAL SUPPORTING TEAM (LST) is the external team of Project Based on Exchange– AIESEC Hanoi. It was formed in order to support the intern service of project. At present, LST is recruiting member to support interns from all over the world coming to Hanoi to work for environment project “Earth & Me”.

Participating into LST, you will have a lot of advantages:

ð Meet and work with many interesting but professional people.
ð Learn and practice many skills such as improving interpersonal skills: teamwork, time management, communication, etc.
ð Improve your English
ð Work in multicultural environment
ð Know how to make a plan and managing skills.
ð Have opportunity to work and make friend with international interns from all over the world.

Although LOCAL SUPPORTING TEAM members are not official members of AIESEC but they have rights to develop as all AIESEC members. So what are your BENEFITS ??

ð Skip the first round of autumn recruitment 2011 of AIESEC Hanoi
ð Explore and enjoy an international environment.
ð Improve your own abilities
ð Free seat in Earth & Me Project events: seminars, camping trips, Green Planet Day – Global Village

Our slogan is “You might not be the best one but be the most enthusiastic one instead”. So what are you still waiting for instead of registering and join with us now?

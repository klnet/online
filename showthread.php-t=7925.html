I am walking in the path to the truth
The path has full light and hope
The path of dreams
The path leads me to the future’s victory

I am still walking
But don’t know the reason why
Why must I do this ?
‘Cause I’m just alone,
Don’t have any friends
Anyone who’s beside me
Anyone I share my feelings
Anyone who makes me happy
.....

And I deep into an abyss
Of darkness, sadness and despair
Is the path to the truth like this ?
And should I better stop walking ?
What must I do ?
What must I say ?
What must I think ?
What must I accept ?
Oh, how crazy like this !

I may deep forever
Luckily, miracle has come
The day when you appeared
Came to me and woke me up
The things you brought to me
Have changing the path I walked
I don’t dare to believe it’s real…

When I feel I can’t be without you
Suddenly, you disappeared
Where are you ?
Where have you gone ?
No, it’s impossible – I think!

Standing up and looking for you
There you are, the end of my path !
I run there as fast as I can
I don’t want to let you go
I don’t want…you disappeared…
You yell: “I’ll be here, to waiting for you.
So if you come here, you’ll find me. I promise”
Yes, that’s the spirit
Now I know the reason for this
I’ll have you at the end of it
My soul is burning
My heart is beating so fast
But you’re here
I don’t fear anything at all
I will come to see you there
And there’s 1 way 2 say 3 words 4 you: “…”
Wait for me, I promise to come!

Ừ nhờ, toàn quên mất là hide đc có cái bài này viết hồi 2 năm trc lúc xin học bổng foundation

[hide]I am a Vietnamese student. I like to study abroad because I want to be more independent and I like to study foreign languages as well as to know more about foreign cultures. I have finished high school in China and now hope that I can continue my studies in the United Kingdom.

The first reason I choose the United Kingdom is that it is the best place to study English. As English has become the international and commercial language, it is understood almost everywhere around the world. In my country, more and more people are using it so the competition between English users is unavoidable. Now the question is not “Can you speak English?” anymore but “Is your English good enough?”. And the United Kingdom is the right choice. Where can you learn one language better than the home to it? The United Kingdom has the excellent language environment to improve your English skills as you can practise it anywhere anytime.

Second of all, it is because the United Kingdom is worldly famous as the centre of academic excellence. The education in the United Kingdom is famous for its high quality and is world-wide recognised. I have learnt that the universities in the United Kingdom not only have long traditions but also excellent facilities and are respected throughout the world. The environment for studying is tailored to suit best for each student and everything that is taught there is very practical and applicable to the real world.

The third reason is that I really like the country itself. The landscape there is beautiful. The first time I saw the United Kingdom on TV, its green of many fields and trees gave me very peaceful and calm feelings. The architecture is awesome with many castles and traditional houses and bridges. When I was younger, I had read about the United Kingdom’s heroes such as King Athur, King Richard the Lion-heart, Robin Hood, Ivanhoe and dreamt of a day that I can go to their home land. And now that dream of mine is even stronger with Harry Potter or the Premium League.

After searching the internet, I find that Wakefield College is an ideal place for me. It has the one-year foundation course I was looking for. I think the course is very important because it will give me the time I need to adapt to the new environment and to bridge the cultures gap. Taking this course will help me on developing my academic and vocational skills. And the price is fairly reasonable. I think I will enjoy the studying there very much.

I am a hard working student. I believe that I have the ability and capability to take the course on successfully. And so I think I deserve this scholarship. With it I will have more funds to travel and know more about the culture of the country I have always admired.[/hide]

Vừa đọc lại thấy ngồ ngộ, có sai gì các bạn cũng đừng có bới móc

Thank bnhiêu chả đc mà, câu kéo tí

I WANT TO BE KEC PRESIDENT

A new contest for you to join!!!

WE ARE ACTIVE
WE ARE MODERN
THEREFORE, WE’RE ALWAYS NEW


Let’s show us who you want to be!

I, Content
2 rounds.

• First round:
- Self-introduction
- Answering the questions.
5 candidates will be chosen for going on.
• Second round:
- Making a presentation of topic:
“What will you do if you become KEC president”
II, Conditions:
1. The competition is only opened for Kim Lien Highschool students.
2. Candidates must be either in 11th or 12th grade.
3. Candidates did attend at least ONE OF KEC’s meetings.

Registration deadline: 12/8/2008.
Competition start: 13/8/2008.


Don’t miss the chance to “upgrade” yourself!!!

Good luck!


P/S: To apply for this contest , pls send an email to kecfam@gmail.com , subject : I want to be KEC president, with the content as follow:
1. Name:
2. Date of birth:
3. Class:
4. Tel:
5. Answer the question: Why do you want to become KEC president!!??

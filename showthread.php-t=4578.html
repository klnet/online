Some members of KEC have desire to take the TOEFL exam to take a scholarship. So, sister Nguyen Thuy Trang, our club's president, decided to set up a TOEFL iBT group - part of KEC for all of members of KEC who want to take the TOEFL exam.
The group is established to help members to pratice TOEFL iBT speaking section. This group is expected to give everyone a chance to learn together - not to be taught. If your friends want to take part in this group to increase TOEFL iBT score, please invite them.

CHAC HAN TRONG CAC BAN SE CO KHONG IT NGUOI KHONG UA GI MON HOA HOC.
CO THE NOI TRONG 3 MON TU NHIEN THI HOA DUOC XEM NHU LA MON KHO XOI NHAT NHUNG CUNG LAI LA MON DE DUOC DIEM CAO NHAT DAY
HOC HOA KHONG KHO NHU CAC BAN NGHI DAU, MA NGUOC LAI HOA CHI CAN CHAM LA DAT DUOC DIEM CAO THOI. NEU NHU TOAN LA MON CAN SU THONG MINH NHANH NHAY , LY CAN SU PHAN TICH TIM TOI THI MON HOA LAI CAN SU CAN THAN TY MI, VA MOT CHUT CHUYEN CAN NUA
CHI CAN HOC THUOC LI THUYET LA CAC BAN CO THE LAM DUOC CAC BAI TAP TRONG SGK VA SACH BAI TAP ROI
DE NANG CAO KIEN THUC THI CAC BAN CO THE MUA SACH THAM KHAO VE DE BIET DUOC NHIEU DANG BAI TAP MOI. TOI GIOI THIEU CHO BAN 1 SO SACH NHE:
-SACH CUA GS.TS DAO HUU VINH
-BO SACH THAM KHAO CUA NGO NGOC AN
-SO DO CHUYEN HOA CAC CHAT HUU COVA VO CO
..........
CON NHIEU LAM
TREN DAY CHI LA 1 SO Y KIEN CUA MINH THOI, MONG CAC BAN CHO THEM NHIEU Y KIEN NUA

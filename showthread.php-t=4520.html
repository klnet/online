"On the Top" is an English speaking competition.
It's the first in a series of competitions organized by Hanoi dethi Club (HDC). Welcome all of you!!!Here are what you may need to know:

- Participants: Every English learner in Hanoi (with a suggestion of registering HDC member list before involving in this game)

- Time: there are 2 rounds:
Round 1: In front of HDC audiences (Saturday 15/9/2007): You will present in from of HDC audience and WE - the manager board of HDC will decide who will come to the final one.
Round 2: - FINAL COMPETITION (Saturday 22/9/2007)
You will have to present in front of a board of experts who are native speakers. And they will decide if you can be "On The Top" or not!!! Just do it@@

- Title: You can choose whatever titles that are suitable for you but you just can choose one title for both 2 rounds. After you register to join in "On The Top" please tell us your title at the same time. From now on, you can register to join Welcome!!

- Prizes: of this competition is really "lovely" and the winners will be presented directly after he/she wins the prize.
1. A certificate for joining the game
2. Cash: - 1st prize: 200 000 VND - 2nd prize: 100 000 VND - 3rd prize 50 000 VND
3. Extra: - Each will receive a CD ROM for practising speaking
- One library card for online internet at Apollo center.

If you want toprove yourself,
if you want to raise your voice,
If you want to be "On The Top",
If you want to be the owner of many lovely presents and respect from others..

IT 'S "ON THE TOP" THAT CAN GIVE YOU THE CHANCE!! HOLD IT!!!!!

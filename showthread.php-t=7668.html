Bản Canon của Johan Pachelbel (Đúng tên bác ý thế này ko nhể ) giờ đây đã trở nên rất quen thuộc với những người yêu nhạc và nghe nhạc. Thậm chí giai điệu của nó cũng xuất hiện trong rất rất nhiều bài hát cả VN và quốc tế, có bài là do sự trùng hợp về vòng hợp âm, nhưng hẳn cũng có bài là cố ý đạo (ví dụ như Công chúa bong bóng còn bê nguyên cả đoạn phần solo thì chắc ko phải ngẫu nhiên ).
Thôi chết cha lan man quá Đây là 1 clip với nội dung là 1 tay chơi cello lên tiếng ý kiến về bản nhạc bất hù suốt mấy thế kỷ qua. Chi tiết xin mời xem và ... cười cùng khán giả

YouTube

[hide=30](laughing) I'm now leaving you with this little story, it's about this piece of music. It's a very popular piece of music, I'm sure you're all know but I'll sing the melody right now, just: "Ba da da da da..."

Yeah, Pachelbel's Canon in D! It's the big hit in the classical world, and I know this because... I'm a geek! (laughing) I know what you're thinking, it's like: "Robby can't be a geek, you play guitar, you're so cool!"... Okay, you weren't thinking that, but I was. (laughing)

Well, I haven't always been this cool because I haven't alway played guitar, I started out on a cello. Yeah, cello is a wonderful, beautiful instrument. It's too cool to be an adult that plays the cello, being a kid that plays the cello...sucked! (laughing) Because there's no way to be cool when the instrument is larger than you. (laughing) When you walk to school with a cello, you're like a wounded gazelle on the Serengeti, man. (laughing) The bullies just smell you coming from a mile away (sniff sniff): "ooh, I don't know what that thing is, but I know I'm gonna break it!" (laughing) But I put up with all of the abuse because I love the music that we played, I loved everything we played in orchestra... except this!

I hate Pachelbel's Canon in D, with the passion! I hate it so much because the cello part is the worst cello part ever written in the history of cello parts. It's eight quarter notes that we repeat it over and over, and they are as follows: D...A... B... F sharp... G... D.. G.. A...

And that's all we got to play! We repeated those eight notes 54 times! (laughing) I counted! (laughing) Because I had nothing else to do. (laughing) I would sit back and listen the violins get lovely melodies, the violas would get lovely melodies, the second violins would get lovely melodies, which would just not happen. (laughing) And the cello, we got stuck with these crappy, louzy, stinking notes! And I became to wonder "Why? Why would Pachelbel do that to us - such a beautiful instrument?" And my theory was he once dated a cellist, and she dissed him really bad! (laughing) And so for the rest of his life, he came up with the worst cello part he could ever think of. (laughing)

It won't be so bad if you don't hear 'em everyday. I know what you're thinking, you're thinking: "Rob, don't listen to classical radio anymore!" I... It's doesn't matter! Pachelbel is following me. (laughing) It sounds paranoid but he's following you, too. You hear him everyday. I don't know, I went to my step-nephew-in-law's eight-grade graduation and their graduation song is the song by Vitamin C... No!

"As we go on, we'll remember... La, da da da...!" (laughing)

So on a drive home, I turned on some classic rocks, some Aerosmith:

"There was the time... when I was so broken-hearted... la, da da da...!" (laughing)

So I got home and I thought I'd clear my mind with some folk-music, no..:

"Listen children to my story, it was written long ago... La, da da da...!" (laughing)

They do Pachelbel just like everybody does Pachelbel just to torment me! I don't even go to Taco Bell anymore, because it sounds too close! (laughing and clapping)

I hate Pachelbel with a passion! I don't even know his first name! It's probably Johan.. they're all name Johan! When you think about it, he's the original one-hit-wonder! He had one hit 300 years ago. It's my cross to bear my entire life. Where are they now, that's what I wanna know. Where are you now Pachelbel? VH1, I Love the 1790's, where is it?? (laughing and clapping) And if he would stay away from music that I love, it would be better, but he won't! He is shameless, he will follow me to the ends of the earth! (laughing)

I went to Horde festival thinking: "No, he couldn't possibly follow me to the Horde festival!" But you know who's at the Horde festival? Blues Traveler. That means that Pachelbel's also at the Horde festival:

"Suck it in, suck it in, suck it in if you're Anne Boleyn or Rin Tin Tin. Make your desperate move and then you're in. La da da da da da....!!" (laughing) So I figure I'm gonna listen to punk rock for the rest of my life, no dice:

"Do you have the time to listen to me whine, la da da da da...!!!!" (laughing)

(Singing) You know I'm getting really bored... 'Cause all songs have the same damn chords! Punk music is a joke, it's really just baroque. Am I just paranoid?... Ah na na...na na...

"I wanna push you around, well I will, well I will. I wanna push you down, well I will.."

"Wa-how, It's been good living with you.."

"My machinehead, is better than the rest. My machinehead is better than the..."

"See the stone set in her eye, see the thorn twist in her.."

"I'm all out of faith, this is how I feel. I'm cold and I'm shamed, lying naked on the floor..."

"He was the boy, she was the girl. Can it be anymore obious?..."

"We're not gonna take it. No, we ain't gonna take it..."

"On your mark, get set, and go now! Got a dream and we just know now..."

"No woman no cry..."

"When I find myself in time of trouble...", Pachelbel's always following me. I'll see you in hell, Pachelbel!!! Oh Pachelbel, Pachelbel, I'll see your ass in hell... I'll see you in hell...Pachelbel...[/hide]

Cho a=b
=>a<sup>2</sup>=ab
=>a<sup>2</sup>-b<sup>2</sup>=ab-b<sup>2</sup>
=>(a-b)(a+b)=b(a-b)
=>a+b=b
=>a+a=a
=>2=1
=>1=0
=>2*1=2*0
=>1+1=0
